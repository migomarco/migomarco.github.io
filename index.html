UFFY

The assignment required us to advertise a product without showing the product. One of the options for our product choice was about shoes.

I made-up company that sold shoes that are fashionable without sacrificing comfort. The name UFFY is derived from the word comfy. I used a baby blue colour to represent comfort.

I chose to advertise a video series on instagram that was a comic ad making fun of fashionable shoes showing the painful reality behind the scenes to such an ad.

Blanc

The french company BIOPHA and NATURE MOI came to me asking for a complete rebrand and a change in strategy to shift their dormant skincare brand internationally. The design aspect of their brand needed a complete change and so did their strategy plan. I decided to create a brand that combines the two sub-brands into one. I made a new company called Blanc to reach a wider international audience while keeping their values and commitments. They emphasised and cared about the preservation of water, the formulation of products are 100% natural, and that they have a minimal impact on the aquatic fauna and flora.

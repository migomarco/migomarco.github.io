THE COST OF EMISSIONS

With an all-electric fleet of vehicles, Volvo is helping drive a carbon neutral future. But how can we get more Volvo EVs on the road in high-emitting CO2 regions?

Volvo will develop a proprietary algorithm that inversely correlates real-time CO2 emissions data with the purchase price of an all-electric Volvo. The higher the emissions in a region,the lower the price for an XC40 Recharge. Helping get Volvo EVs in the regions where they’re needed most

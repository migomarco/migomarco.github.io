EMIRATES AIRLINES


As countries gradually reopen post-pandemic, the expat population continues to grow. Though tourism levels aren’t expected to return to their previous heights for several years, people living far from loved ones are eager for in-person reunions.

While Emirates Airlines is often associated with luxury tourism and business travel, it’s less known that Emirates has long been a top choice for expats, consistently serving one of the highest numbers of destinations worldwide. Given its global reach, Emirates has a unique opportunity to connect families and friends across borders, shifting its focus from tourism to reuniting loved ones. Tourism and business may be slow to rebound, but reconnecting people is at the heart of Emirates’ mission.
